Ocean Freight Services

Our comprehensive ocean freight solutions offer reliable and cost-effective shipping options for businesses of all sizes. Whether you need to transport full container loads (FCL) or less than container loads (LCL), our global network ensures your cargo reaches its destination safely and on schedule.

Full Container Load (FCL)

Dedicated containers for your exclusive use, providing security and efficiency for larger shipments.

  • 20ft, 40ft, and 40ft High Cube containers
  • Door-to-door delivery options
  • Competitive transit times

Starting from:

$1,200 USD

Less than Container Load (LCL)

Cost-effective solution for smaller shipments, sharing container space with other cargo.

  • Flexible volume options
  • Consolidated shipping services
  • Economical for smaller shipments

Starting from:

$45 USD per CBM

Special Equipment & Services

Reefer Containers

Temperature-controlled shipping for perishable goods

Open Top Containers

For oversized cargo that needs top loading

Flat Rack Containers

For heavy machinery and oversized cargo

Ocean Freight Process

1

Booking & Documentation

We handle all necessary documentation including Bill of Lading, customs forms, and shipping permits.

2

Cargo Collection & Loading

Your cargo is collected, properly packed, and loaded into containers with care and precision.

3

Ocean Transit

Your shipment is transported via our trusted carrier network with real-time tracking available.

4

Customs Clearance & Delivery

We manage customs procedures and arrange final delivery to your specified destination.

Ocean Freight Case Study

Client: Global Electronics Manufacturer

A leading electronics manufacturer needed to transport 25 containers of components from Shanghai to Mumbai on a monthly basis.

Challenge:

The client required consistent delivery schedules with minimal transit time variations to maintain their just-in-time manufacturing process.

Solution:

Serene Sea implemented a dedicated FCL service with priority loading arrangements and detailed contingency planning for potential delays.

Result:

Achieved 98.5% on-time delivery rate, reducing the client's inventory holding costs by 15% and improving production efficiency.
