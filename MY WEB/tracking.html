Frequently Asked Questions

You can track your shipment by entering your tracking number, bill of lading number, or container number in the tracking form above. Our system will provide you with real-time updates on your shipment's status and location.

You'll need either your tracking number, bill of lading number, booking reference, or container number. These numbers are provided in your shipping documentation or booking confirmation email.

Tracking information is typically updated at key milestones in the shipping process, such as departure from origin, arrival at transshipment ports, customs clearance, and final delivery. Updates may occur multiple times daily or every few days depending on the shipment stage.

Common shipment statuses include: "Booking Confirmed" (shipment booked but not yet in transit), "In Transit" (shipment is on its way), "Customs Clearance" (shipment is being processed by customs), "Out for Delivery" (shipment is on its way to final destination), and "Delivered" (shipment has reached its destination).
